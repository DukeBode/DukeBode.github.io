0.2. 2020 201-1 Text3

Next time somebody pushes corporate quotas as a way to promote gender equity, remember that such policies are largely self-serving measures that make their sponsors feel good but do little to help average women.

  • corporate 公司的
  • quota 配额，定额
  • gender equity 性别平等
  • equity 公平，公正
  • self-serving 自利式的，谋私利的
  • measure 措施，办法
  • sponsor 发起人，赞助者

下次若有人推动企业（董事会）配额作为促进性别平等的一种方式时，请记住，这样的政策在很大程度上时谋私利的措施，让发起人感觉良好，但对普通女性没什么帮助。

0.3. 2020 201-1 Text4

But it has already sparked significant controversy, with the United States trade representative opening an investigation into whether the tax discriminates against American companies, which in turn could lead to trade sanctions against France.

  • spark significant controversy 引发重大争议
  • significant 重要的，意义重大的；显著的，相当数量的
  • representative 代表
  • open an investigation into … 开展调查……
  • investigation 研究；调查，审查
  • discriminate 歧视；区别对待
  • in turn 反过来，转而；轮流，依次
  • lead to sth 导致，引起，造成，通往
  • trade sanctions against… 对……贸易制裁
  • sanction 制裁

但是它（法案）已经引起重大争议，美国贸易代表开始调查这项税法是否歧视美国企业，反过来可能 导致美国对法国实施贸易制裁。

0.4. 2020 201-1 Text4

Many French politicians and media outlets have referred to this as a "GAFA tax," meaning that it is designed to apply primarily to companies such as Google, Apple, Facebook and Amazon - in other words, multinational tech companies based in the United States.

  • politician 政治家，政界人士
  • media outlets 媒体，媒介业务
  • refer to … 谈到……，提及……
  • apply to… 适用于，应用于
  • primarily 主要地，初级地，根本地
  • multinational 跨国公司，跨国公司的 ；多国的

许多法国政界人士和媒体将这个成为“GAFA税”，这意味着 它主要适用于谷歌、苹果、Facebook 和亚马逊等公司——换句话说，是总部设在美国的跨国科技公司。

0.5. 2020 201-1-Text 4

These unilateral developments differ in their specifics, but they are all designed to tax multinationals on income and revenue that countries believe they should have a right to tax, even if international tax rules do not grant them that right.

  • unilateral 单方面的，单边的；单方面作出的
  • differ 相异，有区别
  • specifics 详情，细节；特性
  • tax 收税，税款
  • multinational 跨国公司
  • grant 准予，允许，授予

这些单边发展在细节上有所不同，但它们都意在（被设计）对跨国公司的净收益和总收益征税，各国认他们有权去征税，即使国际税收规则没有授予他们这种权利。

0.6. 2020 201-2-Text 4

Now that members of Generation Z are graduating college this spring —— the most commonly-accepted definition says this generation was born after 1995, give or take a year —— the attention has been rising steadily in recent weeks.

  • now that 既然，由于
  • generation 一代，一代人；产生，形成
  • graduate 毕业，获得学位
  • commonly-accepted 人们普遍接受的
  • definition 定义，释义
  • give or take 相差不到……；或多或少差不多
  • attention 注意，注意力
  • steadily 稳定的，稳步的

现在Z世代的成员今年春天即将大学毕业——（Z世代）最普遍接受的定义是在这一代人出生在1995年之后（前后出入一年）——最近几周（对他们）的关注度一直在稳步上升。

Joana Lia Ferreira, an assistant professor of conservation and restoration at the NOVA School of Science and Technology, notes that archaeologists first defined the great material ages of human history——Stone Age, Iron Age, and so on——after examining artifacts in museums.

NOVA科技学院保护与修复助理教授乔安娜·莉娅·费雷拉指出，考古学们在考察了博物馆的艺术品之后首次定义了人类历史上伟大的材料时代——石器时代、铁器时代等。

0.10. 2022 201-1 Text2

As the latest crop of students pen their undergraduate application form and weigh up their options, it may be worth considering just how the point, purpose and value of a degree has changed and what Generation Z need to consider as they start the third stage of their education journey.

  • crop n. 一批人，一群人
  • pen v. 写
  • undergraduate n. 大学本科生
  • application n. 申请书，申请表；应用，实施
  • application form 申请表
  • option n. 选择，可供选择的事物
  • degree n. 学位
  • generation n. 一代；一代人；产生
  • educational adj. 教育的；有教育意义的

最新一批学生在填写他们的本科申请表并且权衡他们的选择时，也许有必要思考一下学位的意义、目的和价值发生了怎样的变化，以及Z时代在开始他们教育之旅的第三阶段需要考虑什么。

0.11. 2022 201-1 Text2

This is not to say that there is no point in getting a degree, but rather stress that a degree is not for everyone, that the switch from classroom to lecture hall is not an inevitable one and other options are available.

  • switch n. 转换，转变，改变
  • inevitable adj. 必然发生的，不可避免的
  • option n. 选项，可选择的事物；选择，选择权
  • available adj. 可获得的
  • availability n. 有效性，可用性；空闲

这并不是说获得学位毫无意义，而是强调学位并不适合每个人，从（高中）教室到（大学）讲堂的转换并非一件必然会发生的事，还有其他选项可以选择。

0.12. 2022 201-1 Text2

It has been estimated that this generation, due to the pressures of technology, the wish for personal falfilment and desire for diversity, will work for 17 different employers over the course of their working life and have five different careers.

  • estimate v. 估I计，估算
  • estimation n．估计；判断；评价
  • fulfillment n. 实现，履行；满足感，成就感
  • personal fulfillment 自我实现
  • diversity n. 多样性，多样化
  • diverse adj．不同的，多种多样的，形形色色的

据估计，由于技术的压力、自我实现的愿望和对多样性的渴望，这一人将在他们的职业生涯中为17个不同的雇主工作，并且从事5种不同的职业。

0.13. 2022 201-1 Text3

Nearly 40% of the roughly 350 people who responded to an accompanying poll said they had collaborated with artists; and almost all said they would consider doing so in future.

  • roughly adv. 大致，差不多
  • respond v. 回答，回应，作出反应
  • accompanying adj. 伴随的，陪伴的
  • accompany v. 伴随，陪伴
  • poll n. 民意调查，民意测试
  • collaborate v. 合作，协作

在随后的一项民意调查中，大约350名受访者中有近40%的人表示，他们曾经和艺术家合作过；而且几乎所有人都表示，他们会考虑未来这么做（与艺术家合作）。

0.14. 2022 201-1 TEXT 4

It not placing jobs at risk, to the extent employment protection laws constrain business owners from dismissing underperforming managers, those laws act as a constraint on firm productivity and therefore on workers' wages.

  • constrain v. 限制，约束
  • constraint n. 限制，约束
  • dismiss v. 开除，解雇，不考虑；不理会
  • underperform v. 表现不佳，发挥不够
  • productivity n. 生产率，生产力
  • therefore adv. 因而，因此

就业保护法限制企业主解雇表现不佳的管理者，就算没有把（普通员工的）工作置于危险之中，（但）这些法律也会限制企业的生产率，从而限制员工的工资。

0.15. 2022 201-1 Text 4

Across the Tasman Sea, Australia deals with the unjustified dismissal paradox by excluding employees earning above a specified "high-income threshold" from the protection of its unfair dismissal laws.

  • deal with… 处理；应对
  • unjustified adj. 不正当的
  • dismissal n. 开除，解雇，不予考虑
  • paradox n. 悖论；矛盾的人或事物
  • exclude v. 不包括，把…… 排除在外
  • specified adj. 特定的；规定的
  • threshold n. 门槛，起始点

在塔斯曼海的另一边，澳大利亚将收入超过特定＂高薪门槛＂的员工排除在其不公平解雇法的保护之外，以此来应对不正当解雇悖论。

0.16. 2022 201-2 Text 1

These eggs, which are marking their debut now on shelves for as much as $8 a dozen, are still labeled organic and animal-friendly, but they're also from birds that live on farms using regenerative agriculture －－special techniques to cultivate rich sails that can trap green-house gases.

  • make one's debut 首次登场，初次登场
  • debut n. （新事物）问世，首次亮相
  • label v．贴标签
  • organic adj. 有机的，不使用化肥的，绿色的
  • regenerative adj. 再生的
  • agriculture n. 农业；农学
  • technique n. 技术，技巧，工艺
  • cultivate v. 培育、种植，栽培
  • green-house gases 温室气体

这些鸡蛋目前首次上架，售价高达8美元一打，（它们）依旧贴着有机和动物友好的标签，不过，产蛋的禽类生活在使用再生农业（技术）的农场上——用于培育能够吸收温室气体的肥沃土壤的特殊技术。

0.17. 2022 201-2 Text 2

Even more surprising is that more than half of "unretirees" —— those who plan to work in retirement or went back to work after retiring ——said they would be employed in their later years even of they had enough money to settle down, the survey showed.

  • unretiree n. 不退休人员
  • retirement n. 退休
  • retire v. 退休
  • employ v. 雇用，使用；利用
  • settle down 安定下来，平静下来

更令人惊讶的是，该调查显示，超过一半的＂不退休人员＂－计划退休后继续工作或退休后已重返工作岗位的人一表示，即使他们有足够的钱安定下来，他们在晚年也会工作。

0.18. 2022 201-2 Text 3

Businesses should engage in conversations with IT, compliance, risk, and legal teams to review their privacy policy, and include in the discussion the customer/user experience designers and coders responsible for the company's user interface, as well as the marketers and advertisers responsible for sign-ups, checkout baskets, pricing, and promotions.

  • engage in… 参加……，从事……
  • engage v. 参加，从事
  • conversation n. 谈话，对话
  • compliance n. 合规；服从，遵从
  • responsible for… 对……负责；是……的原因
  • user interface 用户界面
  • interface n. 界面
  • sign-up n. 注册；签约
  • checkout n. 结账，付款处
  • promotion n. 促销，促进，晋升

企业应该与信息技术、合规、风险和法务团队交流，以审查自己的隐私政策，并且让公司负责用户界面的客户或用户体验设计师和编码人员以及负责注册，收费、定价和促销的营销人员及广告人员参与讨论。

0.19. 2022 201-2 Text 4

They chose this particular behavior for three reasons, according to study co-auther Eric Schwitzgebel, a philosopher at the University of California, Riverside: student's attitudes on the topic are variable and unstable, behavior is easily measurable, and ethics literature largely agrees that eating less meat is good because it reduces environmental harm and animal suffering.

  • particular adj. 特定的，特别的，特殊的
  • philosopher n. 善于思考的人
  • variable adj. 多变的，易变的n. 可变因素，变量
  • unstable adj. 不稳定的，易变的
  • measurable adj. 可测量的，可衡量的
  • ethics literature 伦理学文献、理论方面的文献

他们选择（吃肉）这个特定行为出于三个原因，研究合著者、加州大学河滨分校的哲学家埃里克· 施维茨盖尔说：学生们对于这个话题的态度多变而且不稳定；（吃肉）行为很容易衡量；伦理方面的文献基本都认同少吃肉是有益的，因为这样可以减少环境破坏和动物的痛苦。

0.20. 2023 201-1 Text 1

A study last year by the National Center for Science Education, a non-profit group of scientists and teachers, looking at how state public schools across the country address climate change in science classes, gave barely half of US states a grade B+ or higher.

  • non-profit adj. 營利性利
  • address v. 演说，演讲；设法解决，处理 n. 住址，地址；演说，演讲
  • climate change 气候变化
  • barely adv. 仅仅，刚刚，勉强可能

国家科学教育中心（一个由科学家和教师组成的非营利组织）去年的一项研究探究了全国各州级公立学校在科学课上是如何教授气候变化的，且只给了一半的州B+及以上的分数。

0.21. 2023 201-1 Text 1

Another issue is that, while climate change is well integrated into some subjects and at some ages ——such as earth and space sciences on high schools ——it is not as well represented in curricula for younger children and in subjects that are more widely taught, such as biology and chemistry.

  • issue n. 问题，议题
  • integrate v. （使）合并，成为一体；（使）加入
  • integrate into…使……并入，使……成为一体
  • subjects n. 学科，科日；主题，话题
  • represent v. 体现，代表
  • representation n. 代表，体现
  • curriculum n. 课程（复数形式为curricula)
  • biology n. 生物学
  • chemistry n. 化学

另一个问题在于，尽管气候变化已经很好地融入部分科目和年龄段中——如中学的地球与空间科学——但它（气候变化）在年龄更小的孩子的课程中，以及像生物、化学等更广泛教授的学科中没有得到很好的体现。

0.22. 2023 201-1 Text 1

Branch points out that, even if a growing number of official guidelines and textbooks reflectscientific consensus on climate change, unofficial educational materials that convey more biased perspectives are being distributed to teachers.

  • a growing number of…… 越来越多的……
  • official adj. 官方的，正式的
  • unofficial adj. 非官方的，非正式的
  • guideline n. 指导方针，准则
  • reflect v. 反映；显示，表明；沉思
  • reflection n. 映像，显示，表明
  • consensus n. 共识，一致的意见
  • biased adj. 更具倾向性的，偏向……的，有偏见的，偏袒一方的
  • perspective n. 观点，视角
  • distribute v. 分发，分配；使散开
  • distribution n. 分发，发配；分布，分送

布兰奇指出，虽然越来越多的官方指南和教科书反映出（人们）对气候变化的科学共识，但传递更具倾向性观点的非官方教材正被分发到教师手中。

Now, with record-high home prices and historically low inventory, there's an increased urgency in such regulation, particularly among those who worry that developers will come in and buy up swaths of housing to flip for a fortune on the short-term rental market.

  • inventory n. 库存，存货；财产清单

如今，在房价创历史新高、库存处于历史低位的情况下，实施此类监管的紧迫性增加，特别是一些人担心开发商会进入（市场），买下大片房屋，然后在短租市场上大赚一笔。
